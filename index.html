About Page

.My name is David Touchstone, and I am a husband and father of three. Family is at the heart of everything I do, and I enjoy balancing the responsibilities of being a husband and dad with my passion for technology and innovation. Recently, I’ve taken on the challenge of going back to school for a career change. Alongside that, I explore creative ways to earn extra income, like buying and selling vehicles on Facebook Marketplace, which keeps me learning and adapting.

Technology has always been a big part of my life, and I’m constantly finding new ways to expand my skills. From setting up a DNS server using a Raspberry Pi 4 to developing projects with Node.js, Docker, and Visual Studio Code, I enjoy building applications to automate my life and keep the internet safe for my children. I’m also passionate about cybersecurity and am learning JavaScript to explore its applications in the field. Currently, I’m setting up a network server in my own home (homelab) which I can control, implement internet policies and set up my own cloud storage along with video recording of the outdoor cameras.

In my downtime, I love immersing myself in video games like Fallout: New Vegas. It’s a perfect way I unwind in a different world than ours. Whether I’m working on personal projects, pursuing professional growth, or spending quality time with my family, I strive to make the most of every opportunity. My life is a mix of family, learning, and creativity, and I wouldn’t have it any other way.
